Contacts & Pricing

Q.

A.

How long is the tenancy agreement?

Nutnicha Apartment property contract lengths are usually for at least 3 months, however we may have shorter contract lengths available. Please contact for more information Click Here

Do I have to pay a damage deposit?

Yes, a ฿4000 deposit is payable for each room. The full deposit must be paid by the time you check in

How much do I have to pay to move in immediatedly?

Just ฿8000 in total

What does my damage deposit cover and when will I get it back?

The damage deposit covers any damage you may cause to your room; this excludes normal wear and tear. It is refundable at the end of your stay with us and usually received within 28 working days as long as there are no damages or outstanding rent.

When do I have to pay my rent by?

We will be sending out the bill on the 25th of every month, and you can pay the rent before the 5th of the following month.

What payment types are accepted?

We accept all debit and credit cards to confirm a booking. Card payments may be taken online, over the phone or at Reception. We also accept online bank transfer!

What is included in the rent?

Your rent included Wi–Fi internet, Cable TV, Air conditioner, Television, Refrigerator, and Furniture are all included.

How big are the rooms?

All rooms available are 48 square metres.

What size is the bed?

The bed size is 6 feet

Do you allow pets?

No, sorry!

Cancellation and fees

Q.

What do I have to do if I want to cancel my contract?
